About Green Chepalungu

Green Chepalungu is a CBO focused on preserving and restoring the natural environment in Chepalungu sub County, Bomet County. We work with youths, schools, churches, dispensaries and local farmers to promote sustainable land use, tree planting, and environmental education.

Projects We have engage in since we started.

Tree Planting Activities

We have manage to plant over 10,000 indigenous trees in schools, churches, dispensaries and community forests to sort the deforestation issues which has affected our community in a negative way.

Clean Water Project

Through collaboration with local leaders, we protect water catchment areas and educate the community on water conservation practices and advantages.

Our Team Members

Our team includes passionate environmentalists, educators, and youth volunteers who are committed to creating a greener future for Bomet County and the whole country in future.
